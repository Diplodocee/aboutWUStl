Schedules

A guide to Washington University in St. Louis

Plan your year in advance.

Washington University's school year is broken up into three semesters: fall, spring, and summer. Registration for each semester's classes occurs during the previous semester. After the end of classes for a particular semester, there will be a three-day reading period followed by a week of final exams.
